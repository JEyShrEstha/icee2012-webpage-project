Welcome

Hidden heading

The International Conference on Engineering Education 2012 iNEER(ICEE 2012) was arranged in Turku, Finland, on July 30 - August 3, 2012. The main theme of the conference was Contributing to Success through Innovations in Engineering Education.

ICEE 2012 was the 2012 edition of the popular ICEE conference series sponsored around the world by the iNEER Network with 38,000 members in 98 countries. More than 240 colleagues from 32 different countries participated in the ICEE 2012 event. These pages provide information on the past event.
